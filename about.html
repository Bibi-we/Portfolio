About Me

I am a contemporary composer and musician with a background in both traditional Persian instruments and modern electronic production. My work focuses on blending acoustic textures with digital soundscapes, creating music that tells stories and connects deeply with listeners. With over 15 years of experience, I have composed for various media, including film, television, and live performances. My passion lies in exploring new sonic territories and pushing the boundaries of conventional music composition.
